This page contains some resources for the [Digraphs](https://digraphs.github.io/Digraphs/) minicourse at [GAP days
Summer 2025 in Koper, Slovenia](https://www.gapdays.de/gapdays2025-summer/).

Here are the [slides (pdf)](downloads/2025-08-Koper.pdf), and
the worksheet [worksheet (pdf)](downloads/worksheet).

Some data for the problems on the worksheet can be found below.

- [Aubrey de Grey's graph](https://arxiv.org/abs/1804.02385) showing that the
  chromatic number of the plane is at least $5$ in graph6 format:
  [degrey.s6.gz](downloads/degrey.s6.gz)
- representatives of prime $\mathscr{J}$-classes in the boolean matrix monoid:
  [n = 4](downloads/primes-4.p.gz),
  [n = 5](downloads/primes-5.p.gz),
  [n = 6](downloads/primes-6.p.gz),
  [n = 7](downloads/primes-7.p.gz)
- [erdos.s6.gz](downloads/erdos.s6.gz) and
  [labels.txt.gz](downloads/labels.txt.gz).
